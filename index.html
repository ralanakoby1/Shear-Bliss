about us

Shear Bliss was created in 1995 and is run by Gail and Jane Doe to provide the finest salon services. With some of the most advanced hair stylists, the salon is dedicated to offering unparalleled styling techniques along with the most current technologies. Shear Bliss was founded on the goal of gratifying our clientele through the identification of our client’s uniqueness and individuality. Whether it is for a stunning and vibrant coloring, or a hair style that highlights facial features, Shear Bliss and our staff are here to to satisfy our clients' every need.
